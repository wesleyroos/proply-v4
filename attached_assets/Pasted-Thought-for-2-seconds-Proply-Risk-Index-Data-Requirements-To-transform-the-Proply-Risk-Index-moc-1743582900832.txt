Thought for 2 seconds### Proply Risk Index Data Requirements

To transform the Proply Risk Index mock report into a working product, we need to identify the essential data points and their sources. Here's a breakdown of the required data:

## User-Supplied Form Inputs

These are data points that users would directly enter into a form:

### Property Information

- **Property Address** - Full address including street, suburb, city, postal code
- **Property Type** - Apartment, house, townhouse, etc.
- **Property Size (m²)** - Total floor area
- **Bedrooms** - Number of bedrooms
- **Bathrooms** - Number of bathrooms
- **Parking** - Number and type of parking spaces
- **Property Condition** - User assessment (Excellent, Good, Fair, Poor)
- **Purchase Price/Property Value** - Current or expected purchase price


### Property Features (Optional)

- **Year Built** - Construction year
- **Building Materials** - Primary construction materials
- **Roof Type** - Material and design
- **Basement/Lower Level** - Presence and condition
- **Elevation** - Height above sea level (if known)


## Data Retrieved from Partners/APIs

These data points would be retrieved from external sources:

### Security Risk Data

- **Crime Statistics** - Local crime rates and patterns
- **Security Infrastructure** - Area security measures
- **Building Security Features** - Access control systems, monitoring
- **Neighborhood Watch Programs** - Presence and effectiveness


### Environmental Risk Data

- **Air Quality Index** - Local air pollution levels
- **Noise Pollution Levels** - Traffic, industrial, or other noise sources
- **Industrial Proximity** - Distance to industrial zones
- **Contamination History** - Past environmental incidents


### Flood Risk Data

- **Flood Zone Classification** - 1-in-100 year flood zone status
- **Historical Flooding** - Past flooding events in the area
- **Drainage Infrastructure** - Quality and capacity of local drainage
- **Water Table Levels** - Groundwater proximity
- **Proximity to Water Bodies** - Distance to rivers, lakes, ocean


### Climate Risk Data

- **Sea Level Rise Projections** - Future impact models
- **Temperature Extremes** - Historical and projected temperature patterns
- **Building Climate Resilience** - Energy efficiency ratings
- **Severe Weather Frequency** - Storm, drought patterns


### Hail Risk Data

- **Hail Event Frequency** - Historical hail occurrences
- **Hail Intensity Patterns** - Size and damage potential
- **Roof Vulnerability Assessment** - Based on materials and design
- **Vehicle Protection Facilities** - Covered parking availability


### Location Analysis

- **Traffic Index** - Traffic patterns and congestion levels
- **Public Transport Proximity** - Distance to public transportation
- **Shopping Centers Proximity** - Distance to retail facilities
- **Schools Proximity** - Distance to educational institutions
- **Hospitals Proximity** - Distance to healthcare facilities
- **Parks & Recreation Proximity** - Distance to leisure facilities


### Financial Data

- **Municipal Value** - Official municipal property valuation
- **Monthly Rates** - Municipal rates and taxes
- **Levy** - Body corporate or HOA fees (for applicable properties)
- **Historical Property Value Trends** - Price appreciation in the area
